About SLR Public School

SLR Public School is dedicated to providing a supportive and inspiring environment where students develop academically, socially and emotionally. We focus on strong fundamentals, values and real-world learning experiences.

Our Mission

To nurture curious, confident and compassionate learners who are prepared to lead and serve their communities.

Our Values

  • Academic Excellence
  • Respect & Inclusion
  • Integrity & Responsibility

Facilities

Modern classrooms, science and computer labs, library, sports fields and dedicated arts spaces.
